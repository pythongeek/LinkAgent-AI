## 2025-02-12 - Reusable Pattern: Icon-Only Buttons
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons are a common accessibility gap. Using a combination of `aria-label` for screen readers and `Tooltip` for visual feedback (and keyboard focus description) creates a robust and delightful experience for all users.
**Action:** Always wrap icon-only buttons in a `Tooltip` component and ensure they have a descriptive `aria-label`.
